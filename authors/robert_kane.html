Dr. Robert Kane

Role

Lead Author of "In the Hourglass: The Bottleneck Is Still the Human Body—Challenges in High-G Field Accelerators"

Biography

Dr. Robert Kane is a distinguished physicist and biophysicist specializing in human physiological responses to extreme environments. With a PhD from Harvard University, his career spans over four decades, focusing on the effects of acceleration forces on human performance. He has contributed significantly to aerospace medicine and is a senior advisor to NASA and the European Space Agency.

Dr. Sofia Mendes

Role

Lead Author of "Yukawa Interactions and the Value Creation Potential of Their Quantum Fluctuation Variables"

Biography

Dr. Sofia Mendes is a rising star in theoretical and applied physics, holding a PhD from Caltech. Her expertise lies in coupling constants and quantum variability, with an emphasis on creating scalable applications from Yukawa interaction principles. She currently leads a research group at the Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory, developing new frameworks for energy-efficient quantum systems.

Dr. Ian Hartley

Role

Lead Author of "Quantum Fluctuations in Excited Higgs Fields: Harnessing Their Potential"

Biography

Dr. Ian Hartley, a physicist with a PhD in Quantum Field Dynamics from MIT, focuses on the interplay between high-energy physics and practical applications. With over 25 years of research, he has led groundbreaking experiments on Higgs field excitation and its role in quantum fluctuation control. He currently serves as a senior fellow at CERN, contributing to large-scale particle accelerator projects.

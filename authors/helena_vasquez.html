Dr. Helena Vasquez

Role

Lead Author of "What If? Oscillations, Waves, Strings, and Path Bifurcations: A Definition of Thirteen Quantum States"

Biography

Dr. Helena Vasquez is a theoretical physicist known for her groundbreaking work in multidimensional quantum oscillations. With a PhD from the University of Chicago, she has spent over 15 years developing mathematical models to describe the interaction of quantum states across higher dimensions. Currently, she is a senior faculty member at the European Organization for Nuclear Research (CERN).

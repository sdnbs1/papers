Dr. Marcus Lin

Role

Lead Author of "Breaking the Final Barrier: Practical Implementation of Transrelativity Theory"

Biography

Dr. Marcus Lin is a physicist and engineer specializing in high-energy particle dynamics and practical system integration. With a dual PhD in Applied Physics and Engineering from Stanford University, he has spearheaded initiatives to transition theoretical physics into functional technologies. He currently leads the Advanced Propulsion Research Division at NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory.
